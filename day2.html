  1. INSTRUCTIONS:

    • METHODS

    1. Cook Pasta: Boil pasta in salted water until al dente; drain and set aside, reserving some starchy pasta water.
    2. Melt butter/oil in a pan, saut√© garlic, then add your chosen vegetables and cook briefly. Add cooked pasta and mix.
    3. Make the Roux: In a separate pan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it forms a smooth paste (roux).
    4. Add Milk: Slowly pour in cold milk while whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
    5. Season & Cheese: Stir in salt, pepper, herbs, and chili flakes. Add cheese slices or grated cheese and stir until melted and creamy.
    6. Combine: Add the cooked pasta and veggies (if using) to the sauce. Mix well to coat everything. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if it's too thick.
    7. Serve: Cook for another minute or two on low heat. Serve hot, garnished with extra chili flakes or herbs.
